<p><p>The surgical tourniquets market consists of three primary types: tourniquet systems, tourniquet cuffs, and tourniquet accessories. Tourniquet systems are comprehensive setups that include a control unit and inflatable cuffs for blood flow restriction during procedures. Tourniquet cuffs are the inflatable components that apply pressure to limbs to occlude blood flow. Tourniquet accessories encompass various supporting items, such as straps, inflation devices, and monitoring tools, which enhance the functionality and effectiveness of the primary tourniquet systems.</p></p>
