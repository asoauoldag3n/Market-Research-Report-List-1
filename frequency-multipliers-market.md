<p><strong>The Frequency Multipliers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Frequency Multipliers</li><li>Passive Frequency Multipliers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Frequency multipliers are electronic circuits that generate output signals at integer multiples of a given input frequency. The market for frequency multipliers is divided into two main types: active and passive. Active frequency multipliers use amplifying components like transistors to enhance the output signal, making them more efficient and versatile for higher frequencies. In contrast, passive frequency multipliers rely on nonlinear components, such as diodes, to achieve frequency multiplication without amplification, generally resulting in lower output power. Both types serve various applications across telecommunications, broadcasting, and radar systems.</p></p>
