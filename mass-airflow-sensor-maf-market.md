<p><p>The Mass Airflow Sensor (MAF) market includes various sensor types that measure air intake in internal combustion engines. Vane Air Flow Sensors use a mechanical flap to gauge airflow. Karman Vortex Sensors detect patterns of vortices, providing precise measurements. Hot Wire Air Flow Sensors utilize heated wires to determine air speed based on cooling effects. Hot Film Air Flow Sensors employ a thin film heated element, measuring flow through changes in temperature. Each type offers unique advantages in performance and application.</p></p>
