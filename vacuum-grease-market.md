<p><ul><li>Fluorocarbon-based vacuum grease</li><li>Hydrocarbon-based vacuum grease</li><li>Silicone-based vacuum grease</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum grease market encompasses three main types: fluorocarbon-based, hydrocarbon-based, and silicone-based greases. Fluorocarbon-based greases offer excellent temperature stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high-performance applications in vacuum systems. Hydrocarbon-based greases are cost-effective and provide sufficient lubrication for general applications, though they may have limitations in extreme conditions. Silicone-based greases are valued for their versatility and ability to maintain performance across a wide temperature range, making them ideal for diverse vacuum applications.</p></p>
