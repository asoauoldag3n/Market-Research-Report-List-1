<p><p>Magnetic head pulleys are crucial in material handling for separating ferrous materials from non-ferrous ones. The market is divided into two main types: electro and permanent. Electro magnetic head pulleys use electrical power to generate a magnetic field, allowing for adjustable strength suitable for varying applications. In contrast, permanent magnetic head pulleys utilize fixed magnets for a consistent magnetic field, offering reliable performance without the need for electricity. Both types enhance efficiency in recycling and mining processes, ensuring clean material separation.</p></p>
