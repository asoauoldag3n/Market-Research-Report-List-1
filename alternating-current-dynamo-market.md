<p><ul><li>Synchronous Machine</li><li>Asynchronous Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alternating current dynamo market comprises two key types: synchronous machines and asynchronous motors. Synchronous machines operate at a constant speed synchronized with the supply frequency, making them ideal for applications requiring precise control and stability, like power generation. In contrast, asynchronous motors, often called induction motors, operate at varying speeds and are commonly used in industrial applications due to their simplicity and reliability. Both types play vital roles in energy conversion and drive systems in various sectors.</p></p>
