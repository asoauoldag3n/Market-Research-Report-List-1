Zirconia sensors utilize zirconium dioxide as a solid electrolyte, providing high accuracy for oxygen detection in high-temperature environments, commonly used in combustion processes. In contrast, titanium dioxide sensors leverage the properties of titanium dioxide for gas detection, offering sensitivity and stability at lower temperatures. Both types cater to industrial applications, monitoring emissions, and ensuring safety in various sectors, including automotive and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
