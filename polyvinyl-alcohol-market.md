<p><p>The Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) market comprises several types based on hydrolysis levels and specific properties. Fully hydrolyzed PVA offers excellent film-forming capabilities and high chemical resistance, making it ideal for packaging. Partially hydrolyzed PVA, with lower molecular weight, is used in adhesives and coatings. Sub-partially hydrolyzed grades balance water solubility and strength, while low foaming grades minimize foam during applications in textiles and detergents. Other grades cater to niche applications, addressing diverse industrial needs in various sectors.</p></p>
