<p><p>The Earth Resistivity Meter market is segmented into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel meters measure resistivity at one point at a time, making them ideal for simple surveys and basic applications. In contrast, multi-channel meters allow for simultaneous measurements across multiple points, enhancing survey efficiency and data collection for more complex geophysical tasks. This versatility makes multi-channel meters popular in larger projects or research, where comprehensive data is essential for analyzing subsurface conditions.</p></p>
