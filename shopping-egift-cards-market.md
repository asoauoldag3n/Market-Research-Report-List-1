<p><p>Shopping eGift Cards are classified into two main market types: open-loop and closed-loop. Open-loop eGift Cards can be used at multiple retailers or locations, often carrying major card networks like Visa or Mastercard. In contrast, closed-loop eGift Cards are restricted to a specific retailer or brand, usable only within their stores or online platforms. This distinction affects their versatility; open-loop cards offer broader spending options, while closed-loop cards typically provide more targeted consumer engagement with individual brands.</p></p>
