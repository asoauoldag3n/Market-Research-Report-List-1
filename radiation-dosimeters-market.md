<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radiation Dosimeters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LTDs (Thermoluminescent Dosimeters)</li><li>EPDs (Electronic Personal Dosimeters)</li><li>MOSFET (metal oxide semiconductor field-effect transistor) Dosimeters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation dosimeters measure and record exposure to ionizing radiation. Thermoluminescent dosimeters (LTDs) utilize thermal processing to assess radiation dose, providing accurate readings over time. Electronic personal dosimeters (EPDs) offer real-time monitoring, often featuring alarms and digital displays for immediate feedback. Metal oxide semiconductor field-effect transistor (MOSFET) dosimeters leverage semiconductor technology for precise dose measurement and low energy dependence. Other dosimeters may include film badges, pocket dosimeters, and remote sensing devices, catering to various needs in occupational safety and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
