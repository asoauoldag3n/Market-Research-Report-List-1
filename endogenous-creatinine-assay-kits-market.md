<p><strong>The Endogenous Creatinine Assay Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jaffe's Kinetic Method</li><li>Enzymatic Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endogenous Creatinine Assay Kits Market consists of two primary types: Jaffe's Kinetic Method and Enzymatic Method. The Jaffe's Kinetic Method involves a colorimetric reaction between creatinine and alkaline picrate, allowing for rapid and straightforward measurement. In contrast, the Enzymatic Method utilizes specific enzymes to catalyze the conversion of creatinine, resulting in a more specific and accurate measurement with reduced interference from other substances. These methods cater to diverse clinical needs, enhancing the accuracy of renal function assessments.</p></p>
