<p><p>The desk air purifier market can be categorized into three types: passive, active, and hybrid. Passive purifiers rely on natural airflow and filters to capture airborne pollutants, making them energy-efficient but often slower in effectiveness. Active purifiers use ionization or ultraviolet light to actively eliminate contaminants, ensuring rapid air cleaning but may consume more energy. Hybrid models combine both passive and active technologies to maximize purification efficiency and effectiveness, offering a balanced solution for enhancing indoor air quality.</p></p>
