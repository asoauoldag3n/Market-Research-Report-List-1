<p><strong>The Rotary Drum Dryers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Directly Heated</li><li>Indirectly Heated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary drum dryers are essential equipment in various industries for drying bulk materials. The market is primarily segmented into two types: directly heated and indirectly heated dryers. Directly heated rotary dryers use hot gases to evaporate moisture, making them efficient for materials requiring high temperatures. In contrast, indirectly heated dryers rely on heat transfer through the drum walls, preventing direct contact with the drying material. This method is ideal for heat-sensitive products, enhancing product quality and preserving properties.</p></p>
