<p><p>The peppermint essential oil market is divided into two main types: natural and synthetic. Natural peppermint essential oil is derived from the steam distillation of fresh peppermint leaves, offering a pure and authentic aroma, often sought after for therapeutic and culinary uses. In contrast, synthetic peppermint essential oil is chemically formulated to mimic the scent of natural peppermint, providing a cost-effective alternative for various applications, including personal care products and household items, though it may lack the nuanced benefits of its natural counterpart.</p></p>
