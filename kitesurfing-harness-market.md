<p><p>The kitesurfing harness market is primarily divided into two types: waist and seat harnesses. Waist kitesurfing harnesses are designed to sit on the rider’s waist, offering more freedom of movement and a lower profile, making them popular among more advanced users. In contrast, seat kitesurfing harnesses provide added support and stability by sitting lower on the hips, making them ideal for beginners or those seeking enhanced comfort. Each type caters to specific rider preferences and skill levels.</p></p>
