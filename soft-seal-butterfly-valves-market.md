<p><p>Soft seal butterfly valves are categorized into two main market types: flanged and flangeless. Flanged butterfly valves feature a flange on either side for secure attachment to piping systems, ensuring robust sealing and ease of installation. In contrast, flangeless butterfly valves lack these flanges, offering a more compact design suitable for space-constrained applications. Both types provide effective regulation of fluid flow, with options tailored to varying industrial needs, enhancing versatility in installation across diverse operational environments.</p></p>
