<p><p>The marine window market includes three main types: fixed, sliding, and hinged windows. Fixed windows are designed for structural integrity and do not open, providing excellent visibility and strength. Sliding windows offer flexibility with panels that can move to create an opening, ideal for ventilation on vessels. Hinged windows swing open like a door, allowing easy access for cleaning and airflow. Each type serves specific marine applications, balancing aesthetics, functionality, and environmental durability for various marine environments.</p></p>
