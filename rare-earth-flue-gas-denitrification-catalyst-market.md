<p><p>The Rare Earth Flue Gas Denitrification Catalyst market comprises various types, including plate-type, honeycomb, and corrugated plate catalysts. Plate-type catalysts feature flat surfaces, optimizing gas flow and reaction efficiency. Honeycomb catalysts utilize a structured design for enhanced surface area, providing effective gas treatment in compact spaces. Corrugated plate catalysts combine the benefits of both plate and honeycomb designs, offering increased strength and surface area while maintaining low-pressure drop. Together, these catalysts improve the removal of nitrogen oxides from flue gases, contributing to cleaner emissions.</p></p>
